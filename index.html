Am I Not The Main Character?

I think of the events of July quite often. When people rose up to the streets with stones against bullets. To an uninformed audience this might sound like legend and not real; however this is indeed what happened on the ground in the year 2024 in Bangladesh. As the police along with Chatro League started raining fire over the students and general people, the people didn’t disperse- they swelled even more. And yes, they took to the fight stones and bricks against bullets and gunpowder.
